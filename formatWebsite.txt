ashopiy THEME GUIDE:

Primary Colors (Brand):
- primary-50 to primary-900 (light to dark)
- Main brand color: primary-500 (#6366f1)
- Dark brand color: primary-600 (#4f46e5)

Secondary Colors (Accent):
- secondary-50 to secondary-900 (light to dark)
- Main accent: secondary-500 (#a855f7)
- Dark accent: secondary-600 (#9333ea)

Neutral Colors (Text & Backgrounds):
- neutral-50 to neutral-900 (light to dark)
- Text: neutral-700 to neutral-900
- Backgrounds: neutral-50 to neutral-100
- Borders: neutral-200 to neutral-300

Semantic Colors:
- Success: success (green) for positive actions
- Warning: warning (amber) for cautions
- Error: error (red) for errors/destructive actions
- Info: info (blue) for information

Usage Examples:
- Text: text-primary-600, text-neutral-700
- Background: bg-primary-500, bg-neutral-100
- Border: border-neutral-200
- Buttons: bg-primary-500 hover:bg-primary-600
- Cards: bg-white border border-neutral-200
